About us

The main goal of this charity is to help the children of slovakia who have moved to the UK and help learn the skills they need in life for the real world. These classes include english language and slovakian language lessons. This charity also has workshops for both students and adults to join in with and learn communication skills and also helping people. These workshops are all about embracing the Slovakian cluture through traditions and also interactive activities.
